Spaghetti Sauce with Fresh Tomatoes

A wonderful homemade tomato sauce made from fresh tomatoes, onion, garlic, and herbs. Serve over spaghetti noodles, or use in your favorite lasagna recipe.

Ingredients:

  • Fresh Tomatoes
  • White sugar
  • Onion
  • Garlic powder, basil, parsley, salt.
  • Spaghetti

Steps:

  1. Peel and chop the tomatoes
  2. Cook the onion and garlic on olive oil (5 minutes)
  3. Add the remaining ingredients for the tomato sauce and bring to a boil (1~2 hours)
  4. Cook the pasta and pour the sauce on top of it
